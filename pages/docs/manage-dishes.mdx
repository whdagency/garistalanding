#  Manage your Dishes on Garista

Welcome to the world of managing your dishes on Garista! This section will guide you
through creating, Modifying ,and Deleting the Dishes on your Garista menu effectively

## Accessing the Dishes Section
1. Log in: [https://admin.garista.com/](https://admin.garista.com/)
2. Go to the Sidebar located on the left of your screen.
3. Select "Dishes": In the Sidebar, go to the "Dishes" option and click on it to access the dishes management section.
4. Or log in: [https://admin.garista.com/Dishes](https://admin.garista.com/Dishes)

## Adding a New Dish
1. Log in: [https://admin.garista.com/](https://admin.garista.com/)
2. Go to the Sidebar located on the left of your screen.
3. Select "Dishes": In the Sidebar, go to the "Dishes" option and click on it to access the dishes management section.
4. Or log in: [https://admin.garista.com/Dishes](https://admin.garista.com/Dishes)
5. Click on the "Add Dish" Button: Locate the "Add Dish" button, situated in the top right corner of your Garista screen, and click on it to initiate the new dish creation process.
6. Name Your Dish: Enter a descriptive and appetizing name for your dish, ensuring it clearly reflects the ingredients and preparation of the dish.
7. Add an Enticing Image: Images are essential to stimulate your customers' appetites. Select a high-quality image that showcases your dish in an appealing manner.
8. Select the Appropriate Category: Choose the category to which your dish belongs by selecting it from the category dropdown list. This will allow customers to easily find your dish when browsing your menu.
9. Write a Captivating Description: In Garista, you can describe your dish in detail, highlighting its unique flavors, fresh ingredients, and careful presentation. Use descriptive and engaging language to entice customers to order your dish.
10. Indicate the Accurate Price: Enter the price of your dish, using a clear and precise format. Ensure the price is consistent with the value and quality of your dish.
11. Save Your Dish: Once you have entered all the required information, click on the "Save" button to finalize the creation of your new dish.

## Modifying an Existing Dish
1. Log in: [https://admin.garista.com/](https://admin.garista.com/)
2. Go to the Sidebar located on the left of your screen.
3. Select "Dishes": In the Sidebar, go to the "Dishes" option and click on it to access the dishes management section.
4. Or log in: [https://admin.garista.com/Dishes](https://admin.garista.com/Dishes)
5. Locate the Dish: In the list of dishes, identify the dish you want to modify.
6. Click on the Edit Icon: Locate the edit icon, represented by a pencil, associated with the dish you want to modify. Click on this icon to open the editing options.
7. Make the Desired Changes: Garista allows you to modify various aspects of your dish. You can modify the dish's name, replace the image with a newer one, select a different category, edit the description, update the price, or even change the dish's visibility.
8. Save Changes: Once you have made the desired changes, click on the "Confirm" button to apply them.

## Deleting a Dish
1. Log in: [https://admin.garista.com/](https://admin.garista.com/)
2. Go to the Sidebar located on the left of your screen.
3. Select "Dishes": In the Sidebar, go to the "Dishes" option and click on it to access the dishes management section.
4. Or log in: [https://admin.garista.com/Dishes](https://admin.garista.com/Dishes)
5. Locate the Dish: In the list of dishes, identify the dish you want to delete in your Garista menu.
6. Click on the Delete Icon: Locate the delete icon, represented by a trash can, associated with the dish you want to delete. Click on this icon to confirm the deletion.
7. Confirm Deletion: A confirmation window will appear asking you to confirm the deletion of the dish.
