# What is Garista?

## Your Revolutionary Digital Menu Solution
Garista is an innovative platform that digitizes your menus, offering you a multitude of
advantages to optimize your business and provide an exceptional experience for your
customers.

- **Save time, money, and energy** with the ability to modify your menus in real time, eliminating printing costs and improving service efficiency.
- **Personalize service and boost customer satisfaction** by storing customer preferences and regular orders. Offer a personalized experience that fosters loyalty.
- **Analyze consumer trends** to adapt your offerings, optimize your purchases, and manage your inventory effectively. Make strategic decisions based on concrete data.

### Garista gives you complete control over your digital menu:
1. **Create and edit your menu** with ease.
2. **Add information about your restaurant** to captivate your customers.
3. **Manage categories, dishes, and drinks** with precision.
4. **Integrate a QR code** on your tables for simplified ordering.
5. **Manage your staff** and assign specific roles.
6. **Process orders** online and in-store efficiently.
7. **Create attractive promotions** to boost your sales.
8. **Access detailed reports and statistics** to track your performance.
9. **View customer claims** and respond promptly.

Go digital with Garista and revolutionize your restaurant!
