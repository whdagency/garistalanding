# Manage your Qr Codes

QR codes are a convenient way for customers to access your Garista menu directly
from their smartphones. This section will guide your QR codes.

## Accessing the Qr Codes Section
1. Log in: [https://admin.garista.com/](https://admin.garista.com/)
2. Go to the Sidebar located on the left of your screen.
3. Select "Qr Codes": In the Sidebar, go to the "Qr Codes" option and click on it to
access the Qr Codes management section.
4. Or log in : [https://admin.garista.com/QrCode](https://admin.garista.com/QrCode)

## Adding a New Qr Code
1. Log in: [https://admin.garista.com/](https://admin.garista.com/)
2. Go to the Sidebar located on the left of your screen.
3. Select "Qr Codes": In the Sidebar, go to the "Qr Codes" option and click on it to
access the Qr Codes management section.
4. Or log in : [https://admin.garista.com/QrCode](https://admin.garista.com/QrCode)
5. Click on the "Add Qr Codes" Button: Locate the "Add Qr Codes" button, and click
on it to create a new Qr Codes.
6. Name Your Qr code: A window will appear asking you to name the Qr Code
table.
7. Save your QR code: After adding the Qr code name, click on the Save button.

## Deleting a Qr Code
1. Log in: [https://admin.garista.com/](https://admin.garista.com/)
2. Go to the Sidebar located on the left of your screen.
3. Select "Qr Codes": In the Sidebar, go to the "Qr Codes" option and click on it to
access the Qr Codes management section.
4. Or log in : [https://admin.garista.com/QrCode](https://admin.garista.com/QrCode)
5. Locate the Qr Code: Identify the Qr Code you want to delete in your Garista
menu.
6. Click on the Delete Icon: Locate the delete icon, represented by a trash can,
associated with the Qr Code you want to delete. Click on this icon to confirm the
deletion.
7. Confirm Deletion: A confirmation window will appear asking you to confirm the
deletion of the Qr Code.
