## Adding Menu Items

Garista offers a user-friendly interface for managing your restaurant's menu. Follow these steps to add a new menu item:

1. Navigate to the "Menu Management" section of your dashboard.
2. Click the "Add New Item" button.
3. Enter the details of the menu item, including its name, description, price, and category.
4. Upload any relevant images or videos to showcase the item.
5. Click "Save" to add the menu item to your menu.

## Editing Menu Items

To make changes to an existing menu item, follow these steps:

1. Navigate to the "Menu Management" section of your dashboard.
2. Locate the menu item you wish to edit.
3. Click on the item to open its details.
4. Make the desired changes to the item's name, description, price, or category.
5. Click "Save" to update the menu item with your changes.
