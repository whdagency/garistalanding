## Contacting Support

If you encounter any issues or have questions while using Garista, our support team is here to help. Here's how to get in touch:

- **Email:** Send us an email at [support@garista.com](mailto:support@garista.com) with a detailed description of your issue or question.
- **Live Chat:** Use the live chat feature on our website to chat with a support representative in real-time.

## Knowledge Base

Additionally, we have a comprehensive knowledge base available on our website, which includes FAQs, tutorials, and troubleshooting guides. You can access the knowledge base by navigating to [www.garista.com/support](https://www.garista.com/support).
