# Reporting

The Garista report serves as your central hub for gaining valuable insights into your restaurant's performance. It provides a comprehensive overview of key metrics, allowing you to track trends, identify areas for improvement, and make informed decisions to enhance your business.

## Accessing the Reporting Section
1. Log in: [https://admin.garista.com/](https://admin.garista.com/)
2. Go to the Sidebar located on the left of your screen.
3. Select "Report": In the Sidebar, go to the "Report" option and click on it to access the Report section.
4. Or log in: [https://admin.garista.com/Report](https://admin.garista.com/Report)

## Understanding Report Metrics
Upon entering the Report, you will be presented with a visually appealing display of key
performance indicators (KPIs) that reflect your restaurant's performance. These metrics
include:

- Visitor Numbers: This section displays the total number of visitors to your restaurant
or website within the selected date range.
- Orders: This metric tracks the total number of orders placed during the selected
period.
- Revenue: This section showcases the total revenue generated from orders within the
chosen date range.
- Visitor Performance: This metric provides insights into the behavior of your visitors,
such as their average visit duration and conversion rate.
- Last 30 Days' Orders: This section displays a breakdown of orders placed over the
past 30 days, allowing you to identify trends and patterns.

## Visualizing Trends with Graphs
Garista's Report features interactive graphs that provide a visual representation of your
restaurant's performance. These graphs include:

- Revenue Graph: This graph charts the revenue generated over the selected date
range, helping you identify revenue trends and potential growth opportunities.
- Top Performing Categories Graph: This graph showcases the top-selling categories
of items within the selected period, providing insights into customer preferences and
sales trends.
- Visitors & Orders Graph: This graph illustrates the relationship between visitor
numbers and orders placed, allowing you to analyze traffic conversion rates.

## Customizing Date Ranges
Garista's Report allows you to customize the date range for which you want to view
performance metrics. You can choose from various predefined date ranges, such as
"Last 30 Days," "This Month," "Last Month," "This Year," "Last Year," or "Lifetime."
Additionally, you can select a custom date range to analyze specific periods. To select
the desired time frame you have to:

1. Log in: [https://admin.garista.com/](https://admin.garista.com/)
2. Go to the Sidebar located on the left of your screen.
3. Select "Report": In the Sidebar, go to the "Report" option and click on it to access
the Report section.
4. Or log in: [https://admin.garista.com/Report](https://admin.garista.com/Report)
5. Click on the "Choose a date" Button: Locate the "Choose a date" button,situated in
the top right corner of your Garista screen, and click on it to open the status date
ranges options.
6. Select the date: Choose the date range for which you want to view performance
metrics from the available options (Last 30 days, This month , Last month, This
year, Last year, Life time or custom)
