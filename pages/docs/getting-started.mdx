## Signing Up for an Account

To begin using Garista, you'll first need to sign up for an account. Follow these steps to get started:

1. Navigate to our website at [www.garista.com/signup](https://www.garista.com/signup).
2. Fill out the registration form with your details, including your name, email address, and password.
3. Click the "Sign Up" button to create your account.
4. Check your email inbox for a verification link and follow the instructions to verify your account.
5. Once verified, you can log in to your Garista account and access the dashboard.

## Navigating the Dashboard

Once you've logged in to your Garista account, you'll be greeted by your dashboard. Here's an overview of the key sections and features:

- **Menu Management:** Access tools to create, edit, and organize your restaurant's menu items.
- **Candidate Attraction:** Explore features to create job listings and attract top job candidates in the hospitality industry.
- **Analytics:** View insights and analytics related to your menu performance and job listings.
