# Manage your Drinks on Garista

Welcome to the world of managing your Drinks on Garista! This section will guide you
through creating, Modifying ,and Deleting the Drinks on your Garista menu effectively.

## Accessing the Drinks Section
1. Log in: [https://admin.garista.com/](https://admin.garista.com/)
2. Go to the Sidebar located on the left of your screen.
3. Select "Drinks": In the Sidebar, go to the "Drinks" option and click on it to access the Drinks management section.
4. Or log in: [https://admin.garista.com/Drinks](https://admin.garista.com/Drinks)

## Adding a New Drink
1. Log in: [https://admin.garista.com/](https://admin.garista.com/)
2. Go to the Sidebar located on the left of your screen.
3. Select "Drinks": In the Sidebar, go to the "Drinks" option and click on it to access the Drinks management section.
4. Or log in: [https://admin.garista.com/Drinks](https://admin.garista.com/Drinks)
5. Click on the "Add Drink" Button: Locate the "Add Drink" button, situated in the top right corner of your Garista screen, and click on it to initiate the new Drink creation process.
6. Name Your Drink: Enter a descriptive and appetizing name for your drink, ensuring it clearly reflects the ingredients and preparation of the Drink.
7. Add an Enticing Image: Images are essential to stimulate your customers' appetites. Select a high-quality image that showcases your Drink in an appealing manner.
8. Select the Appropriate Category: Choose the category to which your drink belongs by selecting it from the category dropdown list. This will allow customers to easily find your drink when browsing your menu.
9. Write a Captivating Description: In Garista, you can describe your drink in detail, highlighting its unique flavors, fresh ingredients, and careful presentation. Use descriptive and engaging language to entice customers to order your drink.
10. Indicate the Accurate Price: Enter the price of your drink, using a clear and precise format. Ensure the price is consistent with the value and quality of your drink.
11. Save Your Drink: Once you have entered all the required information, click on the "Save" button to finalize the creation of your new drink.

## Modifying an Existing Drink
1. Log in: [https://admin.garista.com/](https://admin.garista.com/)
2. Go to the Sidebar located on the left of your screen.
3. Select "Drinks": In the Sidebar, go to the "Drinks" option and click on it to access the Drinks management section.
4. Or log in: [https://admin.garista.com/Drinks](https://admin.garista.com/Drinks)
5. Locate the Drink: In the list of drinks, identify the drink you want to modify.
6. Click on the Edit Icon: Locate the edit icon, represented by a pencil, associated with the drink you want to modify. Click on this icon to open the editing options.
7. Make the Desired Changes: Garista allows you to modify various aspects of your drink. You can modify the drink's name, replace the image with a newer one, select a different category, edit the description, update the price, or even change the drink's visibility.
8. Save Changes: Once you have made the desired changes, click on the "Confirm" button to apply them.

## Deleting a Drink
1. Log in: [https://admin.garista.com/](https://admin.garista.com/)
2. Go to the Sidebar located on the left of your screen.
3. Select "Drinks": In the Sidebar, go to the "Drinks" option and click on it to access the Drinks management section.
4. Or log in: [https://admin.garista.com/Drinks](https://admin.garista.com/Drinks)
5. Locate the Drink: In the list of drinks, identify the drink you want to delete in your Garista menu.
6. Click on the Delete Icon: Locate the delete icon, represented by a trash can, associated with the drink you want to delete. Click on this icon to confirm the deletion.
7. Confirm Deletion: A confirmation window will appear asking you to confirm the deletion of the drink.
